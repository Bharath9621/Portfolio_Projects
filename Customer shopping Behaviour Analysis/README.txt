# Consumer Shopping Behavior Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes consumer shopping behavior to understand purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and factors influencing buying decisions. The analysis helps businesses improve customer engagement, increase retention, and optimize marketing strategies.

---

## Business Problem

A retail company observed changing consumer purchasing patterns across:

* Demographics
* Product Categories
* Sales Channels
* Seasonal Trends

The company wanted to answer:

**How can consumer shopping data be leveraged to improve customer engagement, increase sales, and optimize business strategy?**

## Key Insights

* Customer behavior varies significantly across demographics.
* Certain product categories drive the majority of revenue.
* Discounts positively influence purchase volume.
* Online and offline channels exhibit different buying patterns.
* Repeat customers contribute a substantial share of overall sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Target high-value customer segments with personalized campaigns.
* Increase promotions during high-performing seasons.
* Improve retention programs for repeat customers.
* Optimize inventory based on category demand patterns.
* Leverage preferred payment methods to improve conversion rates.
